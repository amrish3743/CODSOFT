Elon Musk

June 28, 1971

Innovator,Game Changer

Elon Musk is a South African-born American entrepreneur, inventor, and engineer best known for revolutionizing multiple industries. He is the founder, CEO, and chief engineer of SpaceX, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc., and has played key roles in companies like Neuralink, OpenAI, The Boring Company, and X (formerly Twitter).
